Getting Started

Welcome to the Construction Project Manual Generator

This tool allows you to easily create customized construction project startup manuals for your projects. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. From the home page, click on "Create New Manual" to start a new project manual.
  2. Fill out the form with your project details across all sections.
  3. Preview your manual and make any necessary adjustments.
  4. Export your manual to PDF for distribution or print it directly.

You can also save your progress at any time and return to complete it later by clicking the "Save Progress" button.

Filling Out the Form

Form Navigation

The form is divided into several sections to make it easier to complete:

  • Project Information - Basic details about your project
  • Contact Information - Addresses and contact details
  • Project Staff - Information about key personnel
  • Document Sections - Select which sections to include in your manual
  • Custom Content - Add any additional information specific to your project

Use the "Previous" and "Next" buttons to navigate between sections. Required fields are marked with an asterisk (*).

Tips for Completing the Form

  • You can add additional staff members if needed by clicking the "Add Additional Staff Member" button.
  • All sections are enabled by default, but you can disable any sections you don't need.
  • For custom content, you can add specific information about parking, hoisting, safety requirements, etc.
  • You can upload a site map and your company logo to be included in the manual.

Generating the Manual

Previewing Your Manual

Once you've completed all sections of the form, click the "Generate Manual" button. This will create a preview of your manual with all the information you've provided.

In the preview screen, you can:

  • Review the entire manual to ensure all information is correct
  • Make changes by clicking the "Edit" button to return to the form
  • Export the manual to PDF or print it directly

Manual Structure

The generated manual follows industry standards and includes:

  • A cover page with your company logo (if provided)
  • A table of contents with page numbers
  • All the sections you've selected to include
  • Appropriate headers, footers, and page numbering

Exporting to PDF

Creating a PDF Document

To export your manual to PDF:

  1. After generating your manual, click the "Export to PDF" button in the preview screen.
  2. A dialog will appear allowing you to set options for your PDF (optional).
  3. Click "Export" to create the PDF document.
  4. The PDF will be downloaded to your device.

PDF Export Options

When exporting to PDF, you can customize the following:

  • Page size (Letter, Legal, A4, etc.)
  • Orientation (Portrait or Landscape)
  • Margins
The PDF export feature works best in modern browsers like Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari.

Saving Projects

Saving Your Progress

You can save your work at any time during the form completion process:

  1. Click the "Save Progress" button in the form.
  2. Enter a name for your project in the dialog that appears.
  3. Click "Save Project" to store your progress.

Loading Saved Projects

To load a previously saved project:

  1. From the home page, click "Load Saved Project".
  2. Select the project you want to load from the list.
  3. Click "Load" to open the project.
Saved projects are stored in your browser's local storage. Clearing your browser data will delete saved projects.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

If the form won't submit, check for the following:

  • Make sure all required fields are completed
  • Check for error messages indicating what needs to be fixed
  • Try refreshing the page and trying again

If you're having trouble exporting to PDF:

  • Make sure you're using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari)
  • Try with a smaller file size if you uploaded large images
  • If it still fails, try the Print option and select "Save as PDF" in your browser's print dialog

If you can't find your saved projects:

  • Check if you're using the same browser where you saved them
  • Make sure you haven't cleared your browser data recently
  • Try checking if you're in private/incognito mode (saved data won't persist)

Still Need Help?

If you're still experiencing issues, try the following:

  • Restart your browser
  • Clear your browser cache and try again
  • Try using a different browser
